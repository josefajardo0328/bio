DRAKE

about

Aubrey Drake Graham was born on October 24, 1986 in Toronto, Canada. Drake released his first Mixtape, Room for Improvement in 2006, which sold about 6,000 copies. Drake's estimated net worth now is around 200 million, making him the fourth wealthiest rapper. Drake also starred in the soap Degrassi: The Next Generation in the early 2000s.
